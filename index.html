About Lakshya Institute of Technology (LIT):
    Lakhsya Institute of Technology(LIT),Bhubaneswar, has been dream instiitu-
    tion of the Founder-Chairman Prof.Susant K. Rout.The potential of Odisha's 
    IT skilled manpower and its demand in the IT industry and for re-emergence
    of an authentic institution.Prof. Rout has given his heart and soul to the
    institute i.e . LIT. The college is an endeavour with a practical approach 
    to achieve the target objective of creation of quality human resources.
            
    LIT established in 2016,sets the benchmarks of education with a systematic
    approach to match the best of practices in IT standards in India, having a 
    clear-cut objective to train & upgrade thousands of students for different 
    programs.LIT offers three year undergraduate programmes such BCA,BSC(ITM),
    BSC(CS),BSC(Data Science), taught by professors with decades of experience
    and technical acumen.This instittion aim at 100% Placement success,without
    compromising the semester examination aim by Utkal University.Undoubtedly,
    LIT is not only a unique Institution of excellence but also an  star in IT
    landscape in the state of Odisha.
